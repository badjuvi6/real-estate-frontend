Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How often are new properties added to the website?

A: We update our listings daily, with new properties being added as soon as they become available. Check back frequently or sign up for alerts!

Q: Can I filter properties by specific criteria?

A: Yes, you can filter properties by location, minimum price, and maximum price on the "All Listings" page. Use the search bar to find properties by title or description.

Q: How do I contact an agent about a property?

A: You can use the "Contact" form available in the navigation bar. You can also find a "Contact Agent" button on each property's detail page, which will pre-fill the contact form with details about that specific property.

Q: What types of properties do you list?

A: We list a wide range of properties including residential homes, apartments, penthouses, and sometimes commercial spaces. Our focus is on providing diverse options for our clients.

Q: Is this service available in Ghana?

A: Yes, we primarily focus on properties within Ghana, with listings across major cities and developing areas. We also feature select international luxury properties.
